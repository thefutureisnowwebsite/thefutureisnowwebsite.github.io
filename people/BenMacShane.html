About Ben MacShane

Michael O'Connor, a Frederick born native, graduated from Saint John's Catholic Prep in 1984. He continued his education at Saint Bonaventure University, with a double major in history and mass communication. O'Connors spent nearly 20 years as a local media as a reporter, news director, talk show host, senior producer, and moderator at variation location in near Frederick. In 2006 Michael aquired a job as the business manager at Saint Katharine Drexel Roman Catholic Congregation in Frederick. In 2009 he was elected to be on the Board of Alderman. "Michael is a member of the Community Action Agency, Celebrate Frederick, and Delaplaine Visual Arts and Education Center Boards. He also represented the Alderman on the Blight and Vacant Property Task Force. In the community, he has been a volunteer with various organizations including Saint Katharine Drexel, the Frederick County Workforce Development Board, Friends of Catholic Education, Saint John's Catholic Prep, Frederick Jaycees, Knights of Columbus, and Aspire Frederick. Michael resides in NAC 6 with wife, Tammy, their two daughters, and two dogs." -Found on the website of the Officer of the Mayor
